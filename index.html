About

I am an incoming student in the Department of Statistics and Operations Research at UNC-Chapel Hill, where I will pursue an MS degree in Statistics and Operations Research with a concentration in Statistics, Analytics, and Data Science.

Prior to joining UNC, I served as a Research Assistant at Tsinghua University, working on AI for IT Operations (AIOps) under the supervision of Prof. Yong Zhang. I hold a BS degree in Actuarial Science from the Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE).

My current research interests lie at the intersection of anomaly detection, time series forecasting, mathematical optimization, and agentic AI.
